Course Offerings

Our courses cover a broad range of topics essential for spiritual and academic growth:

  • Theology: Explore the study of religious beliefs, traditions, and practices.
  • Bible Study: Gain in-depth knowledge of the Bible through structured lessons and discussions.
  • Church History: Learn about the development and impact of the Christian church throughout history.
  • Spirituality: Delve into the practices and experiences that cultivate spiritual growth.
  • Religious Studies: Examine various religious traditions and their influence on societies.
  • Geez Language: Study the ancient Geez language, important for understanding historical texts.
