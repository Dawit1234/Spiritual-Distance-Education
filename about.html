About Us

We established Spiritual Distance Education in 2010 with a vision to provide high-quality spiritual education to learners worldwide. With over 200 students currently registered, our institution is committed to offering accessible and comprehensive courses in various fields of study. Our experienced teachers and accreditation from the World Bible ensure that you receive the best education possible. We are dedicated to fostering a supportive and enriching learning environment for all our students.
