Welcome to Our Spiritual Distance Education Portal

We are thrilled to offer a comprehensive range of courses designed to enhance your spiritual journey and academic knowledge. Our courses include Theology, Bible Study, Church History, Spirituality, Religious Studies, and Geez Language. Each course is meticulously designed to provide in-depth understanding and practical application.

Join our community of learners from around the world and deepen your knowledge and faith. Register today and start your educational journey with us. Our courses are fully accessible online, allowing you to study at your own pace and convenience.

We are based in Charlotte, but our virtual classroom welcomes learners from all locations. If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to contact us through our website.
